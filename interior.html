Toyota GT86

Interior


The interior of the GT86 is designed to provide a focused and engaging driving experience,
with a layout that emphasizes simplicity and driver control. The cabin features
a low-slung seating position, giving the driver a connected feel with the car and the road. The dashboard is straightforward, with minimal distractions, putting essential controls
within easy reach. The materials used in the GT86's interior are a mix of soft-touch plastics, Alcantara, and leather accents,
depending on the trim level. This combination adds a sporty yet comfortable feel to the cabin.
The seats are typically well-bolstered, providing support during spirited driving, and some versions
feature leather or suede upholstery.
The steering wheel is small and wrapped in leather, designed for precision handling,
while the instrumentation includes analog dials for the tachometer and speedometer, ensuring clear
readability. A digital display complements the analog gauges, offering additional information like fuel
economy, trip details, and performance stats. There is also a touch-screen infotainment system in most models, offering Bluetooth connectivity,
navigation, and smartphone integration. However, the focus remains on driving rather than
high-tech gadgets, keeping the interior relatively simple compared to other modern sports cars.
The climate control system is typically manual, contributing to the car's lightweight and driver-centric
nature. Though the rear seats are small, they can be folded down to increase trunk space,
making the GT86 more practical for carrying larger items. Overall, the interior is more about
functionality and enhancing the driving experience rather than offering luxury or excessive technology.
